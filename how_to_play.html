How to play

  1. Everybody gets 15 cards
  2. Choose 3 cards. Put the other cards in another stack. The 3 cards can only have 1 spell card.
  3. Put down 1 card. Your opponent must also put down 1 card.
  4. The attack must be higher than the opponent's defence.
  5. The first person to lose all their cards loses the game.
